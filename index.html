What is the YorkTechFest?

A celebration of the York Volunteer Tech Community

YorkTechFest 2018 is a fund-raising community event brought to the City of York in celebration of and through collaboration of the volunteer tech community.

YorkDevelopers and Code York (part of Digital York) are partnering to deliver a unique event as part of York Business Week to represent the best of the volunteer tech sector in the City of York.

In true spirit of the meetup tech scene, we bring together communities on 1 evening in 1 venue at the City of York Council West Offices showcasing a programme of speakers and interactive workshops.

To be delivered in multiple tracks on several stages in a festival format - we represent technical communities including Game Developers York, AgileYork, York Code Dojo, dotNetYork, Front End York, Business Tech groups and more.

Who Are We?

2 partners who share the same goals...

YorkDevelopers

York Developers support, develop and encourage a community of Software Developers in (and around) the City of York. We promote the values of learning and collaboration, helping to develop individuals and groups. We focus on the progression of people, who we hope will assist others in their learning journey. We believe this empowerment of others will support the need for technological and personal advancement in a wider context. We provide the framework for those involved to determine a goal and drive towards it through innovation and collaboration. We are a Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) in the UK. Ref #1177591

CodeYork

#CodeYork is a citywide network of volunteer-led after school coding clubs designed to help children grasp and master the discipline of coding which is basic computer science. In York children aged 10-16 will soon have the opportunity to get coding at their local secondary school and be a part of this growing nationwide initiative. If you are interested in taking part, are a parent or guardian of a child who would be interested in attending or you are a potential volunteer, please contact us on digitalcity@york.gov.uk to learn more about the opportunities this initiative will bring. Alternatively you can sign up to volunteer via our online application! www.bitl.y/JoinCodeYork
